# Growth Templates

Use these templates for repeatable launch and growth operations around Sovra.

## Included Templates

- `launch-metrics-scorecard.csv`: KPI tracking over time
- `experiment-brief-template.md`: hypothesis and decision record
- `channel-brief-template.md`: channel-specific execution plan
- `weekly-growth-review-template.md`: review and next actions

## Usage Rule

For buyer-facing updates to README, docs, templates, or site pages, create one growth experiment record.
